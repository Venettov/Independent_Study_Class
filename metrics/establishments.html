Understanding the Employment Metrics

The charts above show the evolution of total employment reported by employer establishments in Puerto Rico's municipios, based on County Business Patterns (CBP) data.

  • Total Employment: The total number of employees reported by establishments for each municipio.
  • Annual % Change: Year-over-year percentage change in total employment.
  • Annual Change (#): Year-over-year absolute change in total employment.
