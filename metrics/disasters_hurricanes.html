Hurricane Impact and Migration

Puerto Rico is geographically exposed to frequent and severe tropical cyclones, triggering significant socioeconomic shifts and mass migration.

Socioeconomic and Infrastructural Impact:

  • Major hurricanes inflict severe damage to infrastructure, including the electrical grid and housing.
  • Recovery is often slow, complicated by economic instability.
